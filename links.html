Links

• Manulex is an online searchable lexical database that provides the frequency of occurrence of 23,900 lemmas and 48,900 orthographic forms extracted from a corpus of 54 school reading manuals. The word frequency used in Manulex_Morpho are based on Manulex.

• iManulex infra is an online searchable lexical database that provides quantitative information for a set of infra-lexical variables for 45.000 orthographic forms based on Manulex (bigrams, biphones, syllables, G-Ph and Ph-G associations ...). Unlike Manulex_Morpho, the analysis of G-Ph and Ph-G associations does not take into account the morphological status of orthographic and phonological sequences. All the files (including additional data unavailable on the iManulex infra website) are available on the non searchable Manulex_Infra website.

• Manulex Morpho : this site hosts and earlier version of Manulex_Morpho without the search options introduced here.

• Morphiste (under development) aims at providing an analysis of Manulex_Morpho derivational morphology characteristics (bases and roots, frequency of prefixes and suffixes, modifications of bases, ...).
