Spelling-to-sound associations at the elementary school



Manulex_Morpho is a lexical database providing information on the 10,000 most frequently used words in a primary-grade corpus based on French textbooks. It indexes the consistency of Grapheme-Phoneme (G-Ph) and Phoneme-Grapheme (Ph-G) correspondences in the French written language, while also accounting for orthographic and phonological markers of morphology (mainly inflectional morphology). The database was created to assist researchers and educators who need to study and analyze the distributional characteristics of orthographic patterns in the French language, such as consistency, in order to design literacy instruction programs. For further information about the database please see 'Description' tab.

The search function of the Manulex_Morpho website is located under the 'Research' tab. The 'Description' tab provides users with various options to query the database.
